About Me

Hi there, I'm Francine Price! I’m based in the Triangle area of North Carolina, with a background rooted in Spanish translation, linguistics, and localization. Through that work, I developed a deep appreciation for how language and culture shape understanding, connection, and accessibility. It taught me that communication is more than words; it’s about creating experiences that are inclusive, clear, and meaningful for everyone.

As I transition into UX/UI design and front-end development, that perspective continues to guide me. I approach design through a human-centered lens, combining creativity with structure to build interfaces that are both visually engaging and effortlessly usable. Whether I’m crafting accessible layouts or refining user flows, I strive to design with empathy, intention, and respect for the diverse ways people interact with technology.

For me, design is an extension of language — a way to communicate visually, solve problems, and make digital spaces feel more human.
